Grace House

A Christ-centered home for women and children

A Christ-centered, faith-based transitional home, the Grace House offers up to 40 women and children affordable program fees, discipleship and a chance to heal and grow into the women and mothers God created them to be. This home is built by faith, grace and community support.

99% percent of mothers in our program receive their children back into their custody. Our focus is on safety, empowerment, and walking in a new identity. We believe that Jesus restores us to complete wholeness and independence.

Gift Card Ministry

Providing gift cards allows the staff to show mothers how best to shop for their families. We believe proper education in nutrition and homemaking supports the health of the entire family.

Thank you for your support and being a part of this ministry.
