IF YOU NEED HELP IF YOU CAN HELP

Serving those in need throughout the Wenatchee Valley with grace and love.

We call our various ministries "Jesus Places" - providing food, shelter, counseling, community, teaching, and more to anyone who needs it, just as Jesus did. If you're struggling financially, spiritually, or have a problem with addiction, our gracious directors and volunteers welcome you with open arms and will meet you wherever you are in life. We believe Jesus is the solution to every problem.
