Free Food Market

A donation based food market sharing groceries bi-weekly.

By the grace of our individual, local, and corporate donors, we are able to provide produce, baked goods, and dry foods twice a week from The Lighthouse facility in downtown Wenatchee. Families and individuals come from all around the area to receive from this abundance. No ID or personal information is required.

Market Times

  • Wednesday - 1:30pm
  • Saturday - 1:30pm
